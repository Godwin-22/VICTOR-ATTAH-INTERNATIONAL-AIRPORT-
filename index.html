About Us

Gateway to Akwa Ibom & Beyond

Victor Attah International Airport serves as the premier aviation gateway to Akwa Ibom State and the South-South region of Nigeria. Named after the former Governor of Akwa Ibom State, the airport represents the state's commitment to world-class infrastructure and economic development.

With a 3.6km runway capable of handling wide-body aircraft, state-of-the-art terminal facilities, and 24/7 operations, we connect Akwa Ibom to major destinations across Africa and the world. Our airport is designed to ICAO standards, ensuring safety and efficiency for all operations.
